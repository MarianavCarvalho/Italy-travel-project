Why Italy?

One of the most sought-after destinations in the world

The capital is Rome, one of the oldest of the world’s great cities and a favourite of visitors, who go there to see its great monuments and works of art as well as to enjoy the city’s famed dolce vita, or "sweet life." Other major cities include the industrial and fashion centre of Milan; Genoa, a handsome port on the Ligurian Gulf; the sprawling southern metropolis of Naples; and Venice, one of the world’s oldest tourist destinations. Surrounded by Rome is an independent state, Vatican City, which is the seat of the Roman Catholic Church and the spiritual home of Italy’s.

Italy is one of the world’s most important centres of religion, visual arts, literature, music, philosophy, culinary arts, and sciences with names as Michelangelo, Giuseppe Verdi and Dante.
